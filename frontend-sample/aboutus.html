About Us


This webpage is dedicated to showing off the works of Common Good Atlanta students. It acts as a platform for students to display their projects that they have worked on through out the span of their Shakespeare course.


What is Common Good Atlata? Common Good Atlanta provides incarcerated people and formerly incarcerated people with broad, democratic access to higher education so they can develop a better understanding of both themselves and the societal forces at work around them.
